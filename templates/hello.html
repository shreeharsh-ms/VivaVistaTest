Automated Question Generation:

AI-driven question generation ensures a personalized and tailored assessment experience. By adjusting difficulty levels and subject focus, it creates a thorough evaluation process, allowing for a more precise understanding of a student's capabilities.

Automated Evaluation System:

Instant and unbiased grading through AI reduces human error and manual effort. The system evaluates oral and written responses swiftly and accurately, providing fair, reliable results for every participant in real-time.

Real-Time Cheat Detection:

AI-powered monitoring ensures a secure and fair exam environment by detecting cheating in real time. This guarantees the integrity of the evaluation process, protecting both students and evaluators from unfair practices.

Keras

Keras is a user-friendly deep learning framework built on top of TensorFlow. It simplifies the process of building and training neural networks, making it accessible for both beginners and experts.

PyTorch

PyTorch is a powerful open-source machine learning library for Python that allows for flexible and efficient training of neural networks, commonly used in research and production-level AI applications.

OpenCV:

Confidence Detection

Using gaze detection and eye-tracking models (often integrated with OpenCV), you can detect where a person is looking, which can be used to assess whether they are focused

Gaze Detection

Gaze Detection and Head Pose Estimation: A person’s gaze can be tracked to detect if they are looking away from the screen, especially during online exams or assessments.

Object Detection

Real-time Object Detection: OpenCV supports loading models like YOLO or SSD to detect and track multiple objects within a camera feed. This can be useful for detecting unauthorized objects like a phone, paper, or a device placed in the exam room.
